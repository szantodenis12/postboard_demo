## COMPOSITING GUIDE (Affinity)

### Layer Stack (de jos în sus)

```
1. BG layer (BG1-BG6) .................. Normal, 100%
2. VINI photo (cutout) ................. Normal, 100%
3. SMOKE behind (SMOKE1-2) ............. Screen, 50-70%
4. GLASS shards (GLASS1-2) ............. Screen, 60-80%
5. LIGHT effects (LIGHT1-6) ............ Screen sau Add, 40-70%
6. GLITCH texture (GLITCH1-4) .......... Overlay sau Soft Light, 20-40%
7. ABSTRACT accents (SHAPE1-3) ......... Screen, 30-50%
8. FILM GRAIN (TEX1) ................... Overlay, 10-25%
9. TEXT LAYER ........................... Normal, 100%
```
